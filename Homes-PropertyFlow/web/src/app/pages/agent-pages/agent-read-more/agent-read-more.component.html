Get new deals come to you

Want new listings but without all the hassle of cold calling and lead generation, with PropertyFlow’s Property Sales Management System (PSMS) offers come to you. PropertyFlow’s artificial intelligence gives buyers the tools to act on a property quicker and sellers the confidence to get their property to market by themselves, bringing about more deals faster. At any step in the process sellers can send you a request through our PSMS to act on their behalf to close their property sale.

Know the strength of interest in your listings

Now with PropertyFlow’s ViewerStrength™ you can get insight into how your listings are performing against other competitive listings in the area. If a listing’s ViewerStrength™ is weak then it may be time to consider a new marketing campaign to attract fresh interest to drive offers. If a listing’s ViewerStrength™ is average it may be time to tweak your marketing campaign or update your listings details to attract more interest and offers to get the listing sold quickly before its’ performance turns weak.
