View the flow of interest in your property

The hardest part in selling your home is not knowing the flow of interest in your property against other properties, PropertyFlow’s Flowinterest™ solves this by providing a full picture of the flow of interest in your property against other properties in the area. If interest is outflowing from your property, consider tweaking your marketing strategy to increase interest to get offers. If interest is hot in your property, interest is increasing in it which in turn may drive higher offers.

Know the strength of interest in your property

If you have not been getting offers on your property, now with PropertyFlow’s ViewerStrength™ you can see the strength of interest in your property that in turn drives offers. ViewerStrength™ indicates the strength of interest in your property by three indicators, strong, average and weak. If your property's ViewerStrength™ is average, you are potentially not getting enough interest compared to other properties in the area, consider minor adjustments to your marketing campaign to increase interest. If your property’s ViewerStrength™ is weak, it may be time to start a fresh marketing campaign.
