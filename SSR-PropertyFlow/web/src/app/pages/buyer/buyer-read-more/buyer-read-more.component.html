View where interest is flowing across property

Take all the guess work out of your property search by knowing the flow of interest across properties with PropertyFlow’s Flowinterest™. Flowinterest™ visually identifies each property's interest state (hot, stable or cool), along with the primary flow of interest and the ‘hottest’ property for your searched area. Select any property in the map area and Flowinterest™ will identify the primary flow of interest for that property. The thicker the flow line the higher the amount of interest flowing in the arrow direction.

Know the strength of interest in a property

We know it is stressful trying to figure out what price to offer for a property, now with PropertyFlow’s ViewerStrength™ clearly know the strength of interest in a property to better gauge price. ViewerStrength’s™ three indicators, strong, average and weak, indicate interest strength in a property against other property in the area to clearly know price strength in a property. ViewerStrength™ provides price strength guidance to assist you in forming your offer price.
