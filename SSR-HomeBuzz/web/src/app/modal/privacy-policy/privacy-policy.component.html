Privacy Policy

propertyflow.co.nz (“PropertyFlow”) recognises the importance of safeguarding your confidential information. This Privacy Practices Policy ("Privacy Policy") outlines the types of information that is collected by PropertyFlow, the uses for that information, and the ways in which we will protect your personal information you choose to provide us.

PERSONAL INFORMATION

Personal information is anything that can be used to identify an individual, not limited to but including; name, address, date of birth, marital status, contact information, and intentions to acquire goods and services.

COLLECTING PERSONAL INFORMATION

The only personally identifiable information collected by PropertyFlow and shared with and maintained by PropertyFlow is the information a user voluntarily inputs or uploads to PropertyFlow. PropertyFlow will collect personal information directly from you, when you deal with PropertyFlow online or by email.

The type of personal information that PropertyFlow collects depends on the type of service you request from PropertyFlow. PropertyFlow usually collects information such as your name, contact number, email address and password related information, and images of the interior and/or exterior of the property you desire to list.

You may choose to provide us with personal information if, for example, you: register with PropertyFlow, contact us with an enquiry; request us to provide you with information; post information to public areas of PropertyFlow or take advantage of a promotional offer.

By choosing to provide any personal information to PropertyFlow, you fully understand and clearly consent to the transfer of such personal information to, and the collection and processing of such information in, other countries or territories. Any such transfer and processing by us will be in accordance with this privacy policy.

USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

This Personal Information will be held and used by PropertyFlow so that we can effectively provide you our service and reply to your queries or request for service. Information about the kinds of properties you view, make an offer on or claim may also be combined with other users’ information to generate statistics about buyers, sellers and the property market in general.

We may use your navigational information by combining it with the navigational information we collect about other users. Please note these results do not identify any individual, instead they give us aggregated results about the usage of the site. By doing this, we are able to improve the look and feel of PropertyFlow, the content and navigation, and improve the services we provide to you.

Your information may be shared with PropertyFlow’s employees, agents, contractors or business partners, but only in connection with the provision of services and goods you have selected on the Site and are being provided to you by PropertyFlow. Furthermore, we may disclose your personal information if permitted by law or required to do so by law or where we believe such action is necessary in order to protect or defend our interests or the interests of our customers or users of our Site. Except as provided herein or in the event of a merger, consolidation, or sale of all or substantially all of Company’s assets, we will not share any of your personally identifiable information with any third party without your permission.

Notwithstanding the foregoing, given the nature of the primary service offered by PropertyFlow, certain personally identifiable information submitted to PropertyFlow in connection with a property you desire to make an offer on will of course be available to the propertyowner, and with the property you desire to claim as the propertyowner will of course be available to users that make an offer on and you wish to contact.

ACCESS TO YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

At your request, where the law requires us to do so, we will confirm what personal information we hold about you, update your information, remove your information and/or correct any inaccuracies in such personal information if you contact us at personalinformation@propertyflow.co.nz. We will make every effort to respond to your request in a timely manner.

CONTACTING US

We want to develop a continuing and trusted relationship with you. If at any time you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or any practices or dealings relating to this site then please don’t hesitate to contact us at support@propertyflow.co.nz.

SECURITY OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

PropertyFlow will take reasonable steps to ensure your personal information is stored in a secure environment protected from unauthorised access, modification and disclosure. PropertyFlow uses industry standard safeguards to ensure the security of personal information through physical, electronic and managerial procedures.

LINKS

PropertyFlow may include links to other Web sites outside of our control. Please be aware that we have no control over these sites and their privacy policy. Our privacy policy does not apply to these sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy and terms and conditions of linked sites you enter.

HOW OFTEN SHOULD YOU CHECK THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may make changes to this Privacy Policy from time to time, so you may like to check it each time you visit PropertyFlow.
