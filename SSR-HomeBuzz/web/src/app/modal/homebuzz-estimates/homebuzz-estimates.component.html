PropertyFlow estimates

The PropertyFlow estimates are not certified or registered valuations or appraisals and do not involve a physical inspection of the property. If you want a current appraisal for a property we recommend you get in touch with your local real estate agent, registered valuer or property advisor.

The PropertyFlow estimates are an estimated value of what we think a property is likely to sell for. The estimates are calculated from various data sourced from local council valuations as well as published price movement data. The PropertyFlow estimates do not factor in any recent renovations done to the property, nor account for economic changes, relevant regulations or environmental factors that may affect the property value.

The PropertyFlow estimates should only be used as a part of your property research and from which to form your own property estimate value. We in no way recommend using the PropertyFlow estimates as a basis for a formal offer on a property. You should conduct all due diligence when considering to buy or sell a property.
